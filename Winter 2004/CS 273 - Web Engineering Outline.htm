Class
Preparation 		Brigham Young said, "We are prepared for some things, and we receive just as fast as we prepare ourselves." You are expected to study assigned material prior to class.
Homework Homework assignments allow you to demonstrate that you have mastered the implementation details of what we discuss in class. Point values for each homework assignment vary based on the perceived difficulty of the assignment. If the assignment involves programming, the grade is based on the program working correctly; points may be deducted based on the quality of the solution. Non-programming assignments are graded on spelling, grammar, completeness, clarity, and content. Late homework is penalized 10% the first school day and is not accepted thereafter (or after the last day of class, whichever comes first). It is your responsibility to submit homework correctly, including, but not limited to, the correct location, the correct format, and the correct file name. Incorrect homework submissions are subject to late penalties, including not being accepted. You may not resubmit homework that has already been processed. The final homework grade is the ratio of total earned points and total possible points.
Tests There shall be eight section tests to reinforce concepts discussed in class. Point values for each test vary based on the amount of material covered. The first seven tests shall be administered in the testing center on the scheduled dates. The last test shall be administered in the classroom during finals. Late tests shall not be accepted. The final section tests grade is the ratio of total earned points and total possible points.
Grading 75%   Homework

F - 59 and below 		          If the final digit is 7-9, a "+"

Computer
Labs 		Our Linux computer lab is located in AUS 213. Lab hours are posted. Lab assistants provide assistance within specified guidelines. (You may also request a private tutor at the Tutoring Center in the McKay Library.)
Justice
and
Mercy 		Homework assignments and tests are to be completed as scheduled. Do not procrastinate! (See Alma 34:33.) However, mercy is available for a limited number of emergency situations if you discuss your situation with me before (not on and especially not after) the scheduled due date. Otherwise, justice prevails. There will be no routine extensions of due dates - be prepared to justify any requests for extensions.
Disclaimer The instructor reserves the right to make changes to this course outline, including the anticipated schedule, as well as to homework assignments. You will be notified in class when changes are made.
